# Product Guide for kn-biosciences

## Initial Concept
A web application for "kn-biosciences", likely handling business operations, e-commerce, administration, and integrations with third-party services.

## Target Users
The primary target users for the kn-biosciences application include:
*   Farmers
*   Individual Growers
*   Dealers
*   Distributors
*   Large Farmers

## Primary Goals and Core Functionalities
The kn-biosciences application aims to achieve the following primary goals and core functionalities:
*   **Direct Sales and E-commerce:** Facilitate direct sales of specialized seeds and bio-products to farmers and growers, offering a comprehensive e-commerce platform for ordering and delivery.
*   **Logistics and Distribution Streamlining:** Streamline logistics and distribution processes for dealers and large farmers, enabling efficient order tracking, inventory management, and supply chain coordination.

## Key Features
To support its primary goals, the kn-biosciences application will include the following key features:
*   **E-commerce and Order Management:**
    *   A detailed product catalog showcasing specialized seeds and bio-products with descriptions, pricing, and real-time availability.
    *   A secure online payment gateway.
    *   Robust order management capabilities for tracking orders from placement to fulfillment.
*   **Personalized User Experiences:**
    *   User accounts providing personalized dashboards for farmers and individual growers to manage their orders and potentially view tailored recommendations.
    *   Dedicated portals for dealers and distributors, equipped with tools for bulk ordering and efficient inventory management.
*   **Supply Chain and Analytics:**
    *   Integration with third-party logistics providers for shipment tracking.
    *   Comprehensive reporting and analytics tools to provide insights into sales performance, inventory levels, and overall distribution efficiency.
*   **Third-Party Integrations:**
    *   Integration with Zoho CRM for customer relationship management.
    *   Integration with Zoho Inventory for stock management.
    *   Integration with Zoho Books for accounting.
    *   Integration with Google Ads for advertising campaigns.
    *   Integration with Meta Ads for social media advertising.
    *   Supabase Twilio integration for authentication and marketing notifications.
